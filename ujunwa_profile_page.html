About

Ujunwa Onyenkwere (aka Enchantress) blends rigorous philosophical inquiry with creative expression. She is the first-ever female President of the Department of Philosophy at IMSU and currently serves as President of NAPS IMSU (20th Republic). Her work spans academic research, music, fashion, and tech entrepreneurship.

Anthem & Pledge

Verse I
In halls where thought and reason dwell,
We rise where silent truths rebel.
Cogito ergo sum, our creed,
In wisdom’s path, we sow the seed.

Verse II
With minds unchained, we seek the light,
Beyond the veil, through day and night.
Sapientia nostra lux est —
In thought we strive, in truth we rest.

The NAPS Pledge
I pledge, as a bearer of thought and flame,
To honor truth, not chase acclaim,
To seek, to question — not conform,
I vow to think: to speak and to stand —
A philosopher: mind, heart, and hand.
